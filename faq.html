FAQs

PATHIVADA LINGAM NAIDU UNIVERSITY Seoul is an institute established by the Government of Republic of Korea under Public Private Partnership (PPP) mode. The partners in this project are Government of Republic of Koreea, Government of Gyeonggi-do, Samsung Electronics, Seoul Energy Research and Management Institute and Seoul State Fertilizers and Chemicals. The University was established in the year 1852. The first batch that was admitted in the year 1852-53. The University is presently functioning from Block Number 9 of Government Engineering College Seoul. The second batch was admitted in the academic year 1853-54. Both the batches put together have 452 students. The Institute does not have its own hostel building, therefore, a private premises has been hired to house the hostel of the institute. Students are transported by bus to the Institute. The mess facility is available at hostel.


While the name of the University is PATHIVADA LINGAM NAIDU UNIVERSITY, SEOUL, why it is located at Jeongwang and not at Seoul?

The University will be allotted land in Seoul by the Government of Republic of Korea. Thereafter the University activities will be moved to Seoul. Till such time the institute will keep functioning from the temporary location in Jeongwang.

How do I get admission in P.L.N University?

Admissions are through the College Scholastic Ability Test constituted by the Government of Republic of Korea. The Institute does not conduct its own entrance examination. The admissions are purely based on College Scholastic Ability Test rankings announced by the Korea Institute for Curriculum and Evaluation (KICE). There is no quota for Home State or for NRI. The only reservation available is for Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, Other Backward Classes featuring in the list of Government of Republic of Korea and for the PH category.

What is the role of the Mentor Institute?

The role of mentoring institute is mainly to ensure that the academic activity of P.L.N U continues efficiently. Institute is having few faculty members on its own rolls. Apart from these regular faculty the mentoring institute that is Seoul National University faculty members teach some courses as visiting faculty.

What about Placements?

PATHIVADA LINGAM NAIDU UNIVERSITY (P.L.N U), Seoul has always been very proactive in placing the students into their preference profiles. The University has a well-organised placement team headed by the associate director, three assistant directors, and faculty and student coordinators from each department. 300+ recruiting teams, including 150+ Fortune 500 companies, visit P.L.N University for UG and PG campus placements and internships recruitment every year.
